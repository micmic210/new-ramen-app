Mussels Ramen

Our Mussels Ramen is a celebration of seasonal local ingredients and meticulous craftsmanship, designed to delight and warm the hearts of our community during winter.

This dish features a clear, delicate chicken soup seasoned with fine French salt, offering a light yet comforting base. Fresh mussels are steamed with white wine and garlic, infusing the broth with a subtle, aromatic richness. Butter-grilled scallops add a savory depth, perfectly complementing the mussels.

To complete the dish, we garnish it with fresh rukkora leaves for a touch of bitterness and vibrant chives on the side, enhancing both flavor and presentation. The result is a bowl of ramen that is not only warming but also refreshingly light, offering a clean and balanced taste that leaves you feeling rejuvenated.

A perfect harmony of local flavors and Japanese ramen mastery, Mussels Ramen is our winter-special offering crafted to bring warmth and joy to your table.
